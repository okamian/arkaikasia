# ⛏️ Mining System

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (519).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Mining System**

* The **mining system** in **Arkaik** involves extracting **specific ores** that can be **refined** or used to **craft equipment and items**.
* Players must use **appropriate tools** and explore **designated areas** to find various **resources**.
* It is an **integrated system** that supports **commerce and character development**, encouraging **exploration and economy**.

***

## **About Mining**

### **Existing Mines**

* Mineral Mine
* Iron Mine
* Gold Mine
* Diamond Mine

***

## **Mine Variations**

* Each mine **drops specific items**, has a set amount of **HP**, and appears in **different quantities** depending on the map.
* Besides specific **ore drops**, **all mines** have a **common item drop list** that can be obtained on **every hit**.
* Each item has its **own drop rate**, and **all items are rolled separately** per hit, meaning you can **receive multiple items in one hit (if lucky).**

***

## **How It Works**

* **Each hit has a 5% chance** to obtain the **specific drop** from each mine.
* **All specific items** from the mine **have the same drop chance**, but only **one** of them is collected at a time, meaning **one item is randomly selected when the 5% chance triggers**.

***

## **Extra Drops**

* If the player has the skill **"\[Mining] Explosive Mine"** and the **mine's HP is 10 or lower**, the user will have **\[10 \* skillLevel]% chance** to obtain **extra drops** from that mine _(drop is guaranteed at max level)_.

## **Respawn Time**

* **Minimum respawn time:** **1 hour**, but there is a <mark style="color:red;">**30% chance**</mark> <mark style="color:red;"></mark><mark style="color:red;">that the</mark> <mark style="color:red;"></mark><mark style="color:red;">**ore will not respawn**</mark> <mark style="color:red;"></mark><mark style="color:red;">in the following hour.</mark>

## **How to Mine?**

* To mine, you must first belong to the **"**<mark style="color:red;">**Blacksmith**</mark>**"** class.
* You must have the **"**<mark style="color:red;">**Mine**</mark>**"** skill unlocked.

* Rarer or more specific items may vary depending on the mine accessed, but all players will have a chance to acquire the following materials during regular mining activities.
