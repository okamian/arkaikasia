### 10) **Choosing your class**

* Once you have completed all the training steps, you will be directed to the most important part of your character: choosing your class.
* If you haven't decided which class to use yet, simply click HERE to make your decision.
* In this room, you will find the NPCs responsible for changing your class. Choose carefully, as it will not be possible to change later.
* After deciding on your class, the NPC will direct you to the responsible guild. Complete the mission and finish your class change.
