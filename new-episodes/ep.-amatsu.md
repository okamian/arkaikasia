# üéë EP. Amatsu

## **How to Access Amatsu**

* In the city of Zero or Aberta, go to the Captain, click on them and select the **‚ÄúTeleport‚Äù** option, then choose **"**<mark style="color:red;">**Amatsu**</mark>**"**. If you are a VIP, it will appear immediately; if not, wait until the city appears when speaking to him.
* <mark style="color:red;">**Note:**</mark> You need to go at least once through the conventional path in **Alberta** and save in the city to register the point in your teleport.
